About SkillProof ATS

Discover the intelligence behind our modern recruitment platform.

Introduction

SkillProof ATS is an advanced Applicant Tracking System powered by AI. It moves beyond simple keyword matching and understands the semantic context of your experiences. By combining deep analysis of your resume and your public achievements (like GitHub repositories), SkillProof provides actionable insights into how well your profile aligns with specific job descriptions.

How It Works

  1. Data Parsing: You upload your resume (PDF) and provide the target Job Description.
  2. AI Engine Analysis: Our engine compares the semantic meaning of your skills against the requirements.
  3. External Integrations: If you provide a GitHub username, we perform a deep scan of your repositories to find actual evidence of your skills.
  4. Actionable Feedback: You receive an instant dashboard showing an overall match, priority missing skills, score breakdowns, and detailed insights to improve your chances.
  5. Ask Sage: Your AI Recruiter "Sage" is available to answer custom questions about your resume's fit.
